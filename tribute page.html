BHAGAT SINGH

Bhagat Singh was a charismatic indian revolutionary who participated in the mistaken murder of a junior britisher police officer in what was to be retaliation for the death of an indian nationalist.

About the Legend

☛ Bhagat Singh, was born on 28th September 1907, in Rameswaram, Punjab , India.

☛ Bhagat Singh was considered to be one of the most famous revolutionaries of the Bharatiya Independence movement..

For this reason, he is often referred to as Shaheed (martyr) Bhagat Singh. At such a young age, if anyone was smiling just before being hanged to death, it was Martyr Bhagat Singh.

☛ His uncle, Sardar Ajit Singh, as well as his father, were great freedom fighters, so he grew up in a patriotic atmosphere. At an early age, he started dreaming of uprooting the British empire. Never afraid of fighting during his childhood, he thought of growing guns in the fields, so that he could fight the British. The Ghadar Movement left a deep imprint on his mind. Kartar Singh Sarabha, hanged at the age of 19, became his hero.

☛ The massacre at Jallianwala Bagh on 13th April, 1919 drove him to Amritsar, where he kissed the earth sanctified by the martyrs blood and brought back home a little of the soaked soil. At the age of 16, he used to wonder why so many Bharatiyas could not drive away a fistful of invaders.

☛ Then later he went on to search of ideas of revolutionary , he met Sukhdev and Rajguru Then along with the help of the Chandrashekhar Azad he formed the (HSRA) i.e Hindustan Socialist Rebublican Army. Their aim was not only to make our country Bharat independent but also focus on creating a Socialist Bharat.

☛ Then a brutual attack was made on freedom fighter Lala Lajpat Rai at an anti british procession caused his death on 17th november 1928, later Bhagat Singh was determined to avenge his death, by shooting the british official responsible for killing, Deputer Inspector General Scott. He shot down Assistant instead mistaking him for Scott.

☛ Then later he made a dramatic escape from Lahore to Calcutta then to Agra and established a bomb factory. then the british government made measures and passed bill that he threw bomb in the central assembly. Though no one was harmed but the noise and sound it made was loud enough to wake up a sleeping nation from sleep.

☛ After this Bhagat Singh and his friends refused to run and during his trial, Bhagat singh refused employ any Defense counsel.

☛ Despite the pressure from the political leaders, Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev and Rajguru were given death sentence and hanged on 23th March 1931.

☛ Bhagat Singh was only 23 old at that time. And the name itself of Bhagat Singh was enough to stir up the passions of most of the Bharatiyas. He was indeed one of a great revolutionary whose named was written and passed down in our history.
